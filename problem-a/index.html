Gandalf the Grey

A wizard who leads those around him to achieve their greatest potential, Gandalf is an ever-present force in the stories of Lord of the Rings. Always a globetrotter, the grey wizard brings down the forces of evil from the dwarven mines to the elven forests, all without forgetting to let off some stress in the land of the Hobbits every so often.

Reasons to respect Gandalf

  • Gandalf the Grey is the only true source of calm in Middle Earth
  • While everyone enjoys Gandalf's company, they still know to listen when his demeanor turns serious
  • His wisdom has driven back the forces of evil for millenia
